It takes a village to meet community needs

Mary Palmberg, of Iowa City, uses an email list of more than 200 people to help fill community needs. This chart shows how the Helpers Email list works. People on the left made a request or donated an item. They connected with Mary, in the middle, who shared the need with her helpers. People on the right responded to Mary’s requests for help. Five stories are shown on the chart and each has a unique color.

Roll over a person’s photo for more information on their connection to the Helpers Email list.
